## About Me

Most engineers pick a lane. I work across three that rarely intersect: cloud infrastructure, generative AI, and immersive 3D/VR design.

I build serverless, event-driven systems on AWS — the kind that process real business events with near-zero latency. I design and prompt-engineer LLM-powered applications that solve real workflow problems, not just demos. And I model, texture, and optimize VR environments in Unreal Engine 5 and Blender that hold up under headset hardware constraints.

This isn't three separate hobbies — it's one working principle: understand a system end-to-end, from the infrastructure that runs it, to the intelligence layer that makes it useful, to the interface that makes it felt. I gained this through hands-on internships at Tata Steel and the CUTM SDG Program, where I translated technical work into decisions for both engineers and policymakers.

I hold six AWS certifications spanning Solutions Architect, Machine Learning, and AI Practitioner tracks. I'm a second-year B.Tech Computer Science student specializing in AR/VR & Cloud Computing, and I'm actively looking for internship and entry-level opportunities where this combination of skills is an asset, not a distraction.
